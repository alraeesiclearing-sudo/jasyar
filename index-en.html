Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What nationalities are available for recruiting domestic workers?
We provide qualified and well-trained domestic workers from multiple nationalities, including the Philippines, Indonesia, Kenya, Ethiopia, Sri Lanka, and Bangladesh, matching diverse client requirements.
2. Are the hourly and daily domestic workers trained and guaranteed?
Yes, all workers under the hourly or daily system are professionally trained to the highest standards of cleanliness, hospitality, and overall household management.
3. What guarantees does the company offer to the client?
We offer a comprehensive and official guarantee for live-in domestic workers in cases of refusal to work, illness, or lack of competence. The worker will be replaced immediately without any extra charges during the guarantee period.
4. How long does it take to finalize the recruitment process for a live-in maid?
The formal procedures for international recruitment take from 15 to 30 days maximum, with regular updates provided to the client regarding the application status and arrival date.
5. Do you have domestic workers with prior experience in the Gulf?
Yes, we have a specialized division for workers who possess extensive prior experience within the GCC countries, fully capable of cooking, managing households, and understanding Gulf traditions.
6. How are the recruitment costs and monthly salaries calculated?
Costs and monthly salaries vary depending on the worker's nationality, service type (hourly/daily/live-in), and professional experience. We always ensure competitive and flexible pricing.
7. Does the company complete all legal procedures and medical examinations?
Absolutely. Our company handles all legal processing, starting from comprehensive medical examinations, residency issuance, health insurance, up to delivering the worker right to your doorstep.
8. Can I choose and specify the worker's qualifications myself?
Yes, we allow you to review full resumes and profiles of candidates, plus the option to conduct a direct video interview before finalizing the contract to ensure a perfect match.
9. What are the payment methods available to complete the contract?
We offer secure and diverse payment options to facilitate transactions for our clients, including direct online payments, official bank transfers, or cash payments at our headquarters.
10. How can I submit a domestic worker recruitment request?
You can easily submit a request by clicking on the required service buttons on this page to chat directly with our recruitment consultants via WhatsApp and select your ideal worker.
