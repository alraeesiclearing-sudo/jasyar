Frequently Asked Questions

1. What nationalities are available for domestic worker recruitment?
We provide qualified and trained domestic workers from various nationalities including Philippines, Indonesia, Kenya, Ethiopia, Sri Lanka, and Bangladesh.
2. Are the hourly and daily domestic workers trained and guaranteed?
Yes, all hourly and daily workers are professionally trained to the highest standards of cleanliness and hospitality.
3. What guarantees does the company offer to the client?
We offer a comprehensive and official guarantee for live-in domestic workers in cases of refusal to work or lack of competence.
4. How long does the recruitment process take for a live-in worker?
Official procedures for external recruitment take a maximum of 15 to 30 days.
5. Do you provide domestic workers with previous experience in the Gulf?
Yes, we have a special section for workers who have extensive previous experience within the Arab Gulf countries.
6. How are recruitment costs and fees calculated?
Costs and monthly salaries vary based on the worker's nationality, service type, and professional experience.
7. Does the company handle all legal procedures and medical tests?
Certainly, our company handles all legal transactions, medical examinations, and visa processing.
8. Can I choose and specify the worker's qualifications myself?
Yes, we provide you with access to full CVs and personal profiles, and you can conduct personal interviews before contracting.
9. What are the available payment methods to complete the contract?
We provide secure and varied payment options including direct electronic payment and official bank transfers.
10. How can I apply for domestic worker recruitment?
You can easily submit a request by clicking on the service buttons on the page to chat directly with our recruitment consultants via WhatsApp.
